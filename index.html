Antithesis



Antithesis is an asynchronous VR / PC horror game set in colonial America. One player on a computer controls an omniscient witch, spawning minions to maintain control over their spell on the townsfolk. The second player, in the VR headset, must gather the spell components necessary to counter the spell and save the town!
