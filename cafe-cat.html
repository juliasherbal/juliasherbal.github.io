Cafe Cat

Summary

After cats have inevitably taken over the world, you are forced to run a cafe for cats! Try your best to keep up with the cat-o-strophic chaos as you manage orders, take tips, and grow your cafe! Our game is a time management cafe sim where you are in charge of managing customers and handling their orders and tips. What sets it apart from other games in this genre is the variety of customer types to make each day feel differe. Each type of customer will have its own levels of patience, speed, and tip price range. variability in what could happen each day in the game as each customer has different levels of patience, speed, and tips.
